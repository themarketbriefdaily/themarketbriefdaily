1. UK tax landscape for investors

Interaction of income tax, dividend tax, CGT, and pension relief systems.

Learning outcomes

  • Map taxable events by account type.
  • Identify major investor tax frictions.
  • Prioritise high-impact tax controls.

2. ISA framework and planning

Annual subscription allowance, flexibility, and strategic use cases.

Learning outcomes

  • Apply ISA allowance planning discipline.
  • Use ISA for tax-free growth/withdrawal flexibility.
  • Integrate ISA funding with wider goals.

3. Dividend tax mechanics

Allowance limits, band interaction, and portfolio yield implications.

Learning outcomes

  • Estimate dividend tax drag by income band.
  • Locate high-yield assets efficiently.
  • Avoid avoidable dividend-driven tax leakage.

4. Capital gains tax implementation

Annual exempt amount, disposal planning, and gain management.

Learning outcomes

  • Plan disposals around allowance constraints.
  • Integrate gain/loss realization strategy.
  • Prevent forced tax liabilities from poor turnover design.

5. Asset location across wrappers

Place assets by tax profile, turnover, and income characteristics.

Learning outcomes

  • Prioritize wrapper space for highest tax-drag assets.
  • Coordinate ISA/pension/taxable account roles.
  • Improve after-tax risk-adjusted outcomes.

6. Bed and ISA mechanics

Migration from taxable to ISA exposure while managing market and tax effects.

Learning outcomes

  • Explain practical Bed and ISA process.
  • Assess trade and spread costs in transfers.
  • Execute wrapper migration incrementally.

7. Portfolio turnover and tax drag

Turnover policy as tax-risk policy in taxable accounts.

Learning outcomes

  • Measure turnover as an after-tax performance variable.
  • Use tolerance bands to reduce unnecessary gains realization.
  • Balance tax control with risk management.

8. UK fund structures and reporting

OEIC/ETF treatment, distribution classes, and documentation discipline.

Learning outcomes

  • Interpret fund-level tax implications at high level.
  • Check reporting requirements and statements.
  • Reduce surprises at tax filing points.

9. Tax-year-end execution checklist

Allowance capture, contribution timing, and disposal sequencing.

Learning outcomes

  • Run annual tax workflow proactively.
  • Coordinate ISA and pension contributions with payroll timing.
  • Document rationale for major transactions.

10. Compliance, records, and governance

Recordkeeping standards and evidence trails for robust compliance.

Learning outcomes

  • Maintain trade and tax documentation discipline.
  • Prepare for HMRC reporting requirements.
  • Embed tax governance in investment process.
