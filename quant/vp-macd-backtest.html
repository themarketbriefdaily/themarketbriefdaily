The VP-MACD lift over v5c comes from two sources: (1) the volume-price adjustment filters low-volume false breakouts in the trend signal, and (2) the min-variance GLD/TLT split (no trend gate) keeps diversifiers in exactly when they are most needed — during equity drawdowns when GLD/TLT often fall below their 200d MA before rebounding as hedges.
