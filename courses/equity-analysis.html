Equity Analysis

Valuing companies from first principles. Starting from the no-arbitrage foundation of value, the course builds the dividend discount, discounted-cash-flow and residual-income models, the value drivers behind them, the discount rate and implied cost of capital, the link to market multiples, and how fundamental analysis informs an equity thesis.
