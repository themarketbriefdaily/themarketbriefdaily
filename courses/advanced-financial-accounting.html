Advanced Financial Accounting

Financial reporting and analysis from the user's perspective — the full CFA Level 1 Financial Statement Analysis syllabus. Each lesson develops the accounting standard, then turns it on its head to ask how the choice of policy distorts the ratios and what that tells you about a company's real performance and earnings quality.
