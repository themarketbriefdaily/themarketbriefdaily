Research foundation

Built on academic rigour.

The AI Day‑Trader implements a regime‑switching score‑driven Student‑t copula with macro‑conditioned transitions.

"The correlation between equities and government bonds is a cornerstone of modern portfolio construction … however, this hedge is neither constant nor reliable."

Key contributions

  • First copula model combining score‑driven updates, regime switching, and MIDAS‑filtered macro factors.
  • Two complementary specifications: medium‑horizon (Model A) and daily asymmetric (Model B).
  • Empirically validated 1990–2025 with robust out‑of‑sample benefits.

Static correlation models fail in crises. This bot adapts in real time to inflation shocks, flight‑to‑quality, and monetary policy shifts.
