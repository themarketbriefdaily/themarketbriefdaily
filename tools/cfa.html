Insider tips

Exam strategy from
a CFA candidate.

Genuine, actionable advice for the Level 1 exam — from someone who's been through the curriculum.

Ethics is the highest-weighted topic

At 15–20% of the exam, Ethics is the single most heavily weighted topic. Never guess on ethics — read every option carefully. The answer is almost always about disclosure, independence, or materiality. Flag ethical scenarios you're unsure about and return to them.

FRA + Quant = ~30% of the exam

Financial Reporting & Analysis combined with Quantitative Methods accounts for roughly 30% of the Level 1 exam. These are learnable with repetition — use this tool daily for 4 weeks and you will see the patterns emerge. Focus on ratio analysis, DCF mechanics, and probability concepts.

Fixed Income is conceptually heavy

Fixed Income is conceptually heavy but formula-light relative to other topics. Focus on duration, convexity, and the yield curve relationships. These concepts appear in portfolio management too, so mastering them here pays dividends later in the curriculum.
