03 / FAQ

Common
questions.

Anything we haven't answered? Email us — we reply within one business day.

Is this regulated investment advice?

No. The Market Brief Daily publishes educational research for independent investors, finance students, and IFA practices. We are not authorised by the FCA to provide regulated investment advice. Everything published — including model portfolio positions — is illustrative for research and learning purposes. Treat it like a research note from a buy-side analyst, not a recommendation from an adviser.

How do the model portfolios work — are they real money?

No. The four model portfolios (MBD-MACRO, STRUCT, SUPPLY, FACT) are illustrative simulations tracked publicly with full transparency. Returns are computed using observable market prices, but no client capital is allocated. The point is to demonstrate the methodology — read the methodology page for the full construction rules.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All paid tiers (Professional and Institutional) are billed monthly or annually and you can cancel anytime from the dashboard. No phone calls, no retention pressure. Annual plans are non-refundable beyond 14 days but you keep access until the term ends.

What payment methods are accepted?

Stripe handles all payments — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank debit via SEPA / BACS. Institutional clients can pay by invoice on net-30 terms. Pricing displayed in £ but USD / EUR are auto-charged at the prevailing rate.

How is the AI day-trader different from a signal service?

It's an open-book research experiment, not a signal subscription. The model trades a paper account using a documented strategy (RS-GAS-t copula on macro factors). Every trade is published with full reasoning. The point is transparency, not actionable signals — please don't copy these trades into a live account without doing your own due diligence and risk-sizing work.

Do you offer student / academic discounts?

Yes — students with a valid .ac.uk / .edu email get Professional at £9/month. Email us from your institution address and we'll send the discount code. Active CFA candidates with a verified ID also qualify.

What's in the Institutional tier specifically?

Bespoke research (one 12-page institutional report per month — you choose the topic), direct analyst access via email and a monthly 30-min call, REST API access to the macro signal dashboard with webhook delivery, 10 team seats with SSO, a compliance pack (audit trail, disclaimers, MiFID-II-style documentation), and white-label PDF report rights for client distribution.

I'm on Substack — what changes if I upgrade?

The free Substack continues to deliver the daily brief. Professional adds access to the website's full research archive, model portfolio attribution, the AI day-trader alerts, and unlimited tool access. You'll log in via marketbriefdaily.com using the email you subscribed with.
