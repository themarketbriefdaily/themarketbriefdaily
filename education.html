IMC Zero-to-Pass Bootcamp (for complete beginners)

If you are starting from zero, use this sequence before moving lesson-by-lesson: first learn language and formulas, then exam application, then timed practice with error correction.

12-Week cadence
  • Weeks 1–4: Unit 1 core concepts + glossary drills.
  • Weeks 5–8: Unit 2 portfolio/application blocks + worked examples.
  • Weeks 9–10: Mixed-topic retrieval practice and weak-area rebuild.
  • Weeks 11–12: Full mocks, timing, and final error-log revision.
Daily study template (90–120 min)
  • 20 min: vocabulary + formula flashcards.
  • 35 min: one lesson deep read with notes in your own words.
  • 25 min: calculations + one worked case write-up.
  • 15 min: quiz yourself from memory; update mistake log.
Exam method for every question
  • Define objective and identify the IMC domain tested.
  • Select the framework/formula and compute clearly.
  • Interpret result in client/regulatory language.
  • State one risk/control that could invalidate your answer.
Core readings to cycle weekly
  • CISI IMC official Unit 1 and Unit 2 learning outcomes.
  • FCA handbook summary pages on conduct and client duty.
  • Basic accounting and valuation primers (income statement, cash flow, TVM).
  • Past-paper style practice sets with rationale review.

IMC Complete-Course Mastery Framework (100% target pathway)

This section enforces full IMC coverage and mastery gating so no syllabus area is skipped before final exam attempts.

Unit 1 full coverage map
  • Regulatory structure, perimeter, and authorisation checks.
  • Conduct, suitability, vulnerable clients, and client outcomes.
  • AML/sanctions/market-abuse controls and escalation workflow.
  • Economics, macro transmission, and inflation/real-return logic.
  • Accounting interpretation, statement linkage, and cash quality.
  • TVM, discounting, portfolio risk, and diversification math.
Unit 2 full coverage map
  • Fact-find, IPS drafting, and objective/constraint quantification.
  • Strategic allocation, rebalancing, and risk-budget controls.
  • Equity, fixed-income, derivative, fund, and alternatives workflows.
  • Implementation frictions: fees, liquidity, taxes, collateral.
  • Attribution, manager review, and governance decision controls.
  • Integrated case-answer structure under timed conditions.
Mandatory novice outputs
  1. Create a personal glossary of 250+ IMC terms.
  2. Build a formula sheet and complete 200+ calculation drills.
  3. Write 60+ structured case responses using exam command words.
  4. Maintain an error log with correction notes and retest dates.
Mock-exam protocol
  1. Complete 6 full timed mocks (3 per unit equivalent weighting).
  2. No final booking unless last 3 mocks average at least 85%.
  3. Any domain below 80% triggers mandatory rebuild and retest.
  4. Final week target: mixed-domain timed sets at 90%+ accuracy.
