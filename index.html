About

Independent market research

The mission

The Market Brief Daily is an independent research publication delivering institutional-grade market analysis without bias or conflicts of interest — written by a single analyst, not a committee.

What I cover

  • Market Structure — Order flow dynamics, liquidity patterns, and microstructure effects
  • Physical Supply — Commodity constraints, production capacity, and logistics bottlenecks
  • Macro Transmission — How policy, supply shocks, and financial conditions propagate through markets
  • Investment Strategy — Multi-asset allocation insights for regime-aware portfolio construction

The approach

Quantitative rigor combined with qualitative judgment. Every analysis is grounded in defensible frameworks and real-world market mechanics — not ideology or consensus. When the framework is wrong, I say so.
