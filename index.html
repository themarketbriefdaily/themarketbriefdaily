About the author

MB

The Market Brief Daily

Solo-operator research publication

I write daily briefs on market structure, physical supply, and macro transmission — the parts of the market that consensus commentary tends to skim past. The goal isn't to add to the noise; it's to publish frameworks I'd actually use to run capital.

My background combines buy-side multi-asset research with quantitative modelling on commodity supply chains and rate-curve dynamics. The brief is the byproduct of that work — what I read, what I model, what changes my mind.

What's distinctive: No sell-side house view, no fund flow to defend, no clients to placate. When I'm wrong I say so in the next brief. When something doesn't fit the dominant narrative, that's usually where I focus.
