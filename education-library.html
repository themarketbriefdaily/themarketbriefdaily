Curriculum Source Standards

  • CFA Institute — curriculum topic architecture, learning outcome statements, and ethics framework.
  • Kaplan Schweser — topic sequencing conventions, concept maps, and summary conventions.
  • Academic literature — Fama (1970, 1991), Sharpe (1991), Markowitz (1952), Fama-French (1992, 1993, 2015), Carhart (1997), Bengen (1994), Black & Litterman (1992).
  • Institutional research — BIS Quarterly Reviews, SPIVA persistence scorecards, CPMI-IOSCO guidance, Vanguard and Dimensional research.
  • UK regulatory sources — HMRC ISA, CGT, dividend, pension and self-assessment guidance; FCA consumer investing and scam-awareness publications. Tax figures verified for the 2026/27 tax year.
