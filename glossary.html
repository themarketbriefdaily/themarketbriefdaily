Investment glossary

Search common market terms used across our daily briefings and strategy notes.

Carry trade

Borrowing in a lower-yielding currency or asset to fund a higher-yielding position, capturing spread while managing drawdown risk.

Duration

A measure of bond price sensitivity to interest-rate changes; higher duration means larger price moves for a given yield shift.

Term premium

Additional compensation investors require to hold longer-dated bonds versus rolling short-term debt.

Gamma squeeze

Acceleration in underlying price movement driven by dealer hedging flows linked to options positioning.

Backwardation

Commodity futures structure where near-dated contracts trade above longer maturities, often signaling prompt scarcity.

Credit spread

Yield differential between corporate and government debt that reflects perceived default and liquidity risk.
