01 Strategy Overview

The Rotational Fund is a proprietary thematic strategy that allocates across five structural mega-trends, dynamically adjusting weights based on momentum, macro regime signals, and relative valuation. Unlike passive multi-asset strategies, it makes active, high-conviction rotational calls between themes as economic cycles evolve.

The portfolio balances secular growth themes (AI infrastructure, quantum computing, cybersecurity) with defensive real-asset exposure (precious metals) and ESG transition plays (clean energy, European consumer staples). Theme weights are reviewed monthly using a quantitative scoring model incorporating price momentum, earnings revisions, and macro sensitivity.

02 The Five Themes

01 AI Infrastructure Cybersecurity fabric, cloud computing backbone, and enterprise AI platforms benefiting from the secular capex cycle in artificial intelligence. Core holdings: Fortinet, CrowdStrike, Cloudflare, MKS Instruments.
02 Quantum Computing Early-stage but rapidly developing exposure to quantum hardware and algorithms — positioned ahead of the anticipated inflection in error-corrected quantum advantage.
03 Energy Transition Global clean energy transition via iShares Global Clean Energy ETF (ICLN) — capturing solar, wind, and grid infrastructure amid the decarbonisation supercycle.
04 Precious Metals Gold and silver as monetary reserves and inflation hedges. Structural tailwind from de-dollarisation trends and central bank accumulation cycles.
05 EU Consumer Staples iShares MSCI Europe Consumer Staples — a defensive anchor with attractive valuations relative to US peers and resilient earnings in recessionary environments.
